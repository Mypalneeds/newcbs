01

Brief & Consultation

Frequently Asked

Common Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?
Our minimum order is typically 50 units for most product categories, though this varies by product type. We cater to both small- and large-scale requirements — contact us to discuss your specific needs.
How long does production take?
Standard turnaround is 10–14 business days from artwork approval. For urgent campaigns, we offer a 5–7 day express service for most products. Complex custom items may require additional lead time.
Do you deliver outside Lagos?
Yes. We deliver across Nigeria — Abuja, Port Harcourt, Kano, Ibadan, and all major cities. We also fulfil orders across West Africa via our ECS Group logistics infrastructure.
Can I request a product sample before ordering?
Absolutely. We strongly encourage sampling for new clients. Pre-production samples can be arranged for most product categories. Contact us to request a sample kit relevant to your campaign type.
What branding methods do you use?
We use embroidery, screen printing, digital printing, laser engraving, debossing, heat transfer, and sublimation printing — the method is chosen based on the product and your brand guidelines.
Do you source products internationally?
Yes. Creative Branding Solutions has robust global sourcing capability — we procure from trusted suppliers in Asia, Europe, and locally in Nigeria to ensure the best quality at the most competitive prices for your brief.
